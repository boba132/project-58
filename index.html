My Favorite Hobby is Drawing

About

Drawing has always been a sort of relaxing activity, but with extracurricular classes and the soon to return school days, I barely have time to draw anymore. I usually stay up late so I can, but not so late that I'll be too tired to play soccer, or to focus on the lesson in piano or coding. I usually draw landscapes and flowers, and sometimes portraits with semi-realism.
